• This document was a draft version of the official announcement of Britain's nuclear tests in 1957. It was passed to various senior military and political figures to make sure that they were happy with what it said before it was given to the press.
  • Even in 1957 there were some concerns about the amount of nuclear testing going on in the Pacific.
  • It was known that nuclear contamination could cause genetic mutations in animals and even humans. This knowledge inspired the Japanese science fiction monster Godzilla. There were several US films which also took on this theme in the 1950s and 1960s.
  • This document was accompanied by notes about what questions could be answered after the initial announcement.
