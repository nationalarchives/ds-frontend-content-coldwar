• This cartoon was published in the British newspaper the Daily Telegraph on January 30th 1973.
  • Peace talks over Vietnam had been going on for years in Paris, with Britain and the USSR acting as mediators.
  • On January 23rd 1973 Nixon announced that an agreement had been reached and that US troops would be withdrawing from Vietnam from January 28th. They would all be out in 60 days.
  • Nixon called the deal 'Peace With Honour'. However, most people regarded it as an admission that the USA was simply unable to win the war.
  • This feeling was reinforced two years later when South Vietnam was invaded and became part of the Communist republic of Vietnam.
