You probably already know that the Cold War was complicated. One of the problems is that historians cannot all agree why it started. There are even arguments about when it started!

Most historians say the Cold War began soon after the end of World War 2 in 1945. However, relations between the USSR and Britain and the USA were often strained before that date. This Gallery and the next one look at this issue.
